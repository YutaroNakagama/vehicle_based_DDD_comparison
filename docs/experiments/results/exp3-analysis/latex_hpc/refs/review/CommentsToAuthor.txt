Comments to author (Associate Editor)
=====================================

The topic of the paper is interesting and relevant in the
field of IV. However, the paper needs many major changes to
meet the quality of the conference. The structure is mostly
unclear and the results are not clearly presented.

First, the authors mention that the aim of the paper is to
develop a DDD methodology. But then they write that they
create a framework to compare Related Work methods. They
also write that they implement three existing methods and
another concise method: the authors mention the four
methods "SVMA", "SVMW", "LSTM" and "RF". However, they do
not explain the abbreviation, nor do they cite the related
work. The description of their own method "RF" is
unfortunately only 7 lines long, which makes it difficult
to compare with the other methods.

It would be desirable to devote a lot more space to the
author's method and possibly leave out some references
instead (there is already a lot of space left blank
anyway). 

A future version of the paper should include a detailed
explanation of the real innovation and a clear explanation
of the scientific contribution.
